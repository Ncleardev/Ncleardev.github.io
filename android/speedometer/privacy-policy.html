Privacy Policy for GPS Speedometer Pro

GPS Speedometer Pro is a mobile application developed by Nclear PROJECT.

Nclear PROJECT does not collect or share any personal information.

Non-personal data, such as app usage, may be collected automatically by Google Play Services. More information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Location permission is used to access the device's GPS sensor to obtain current speed and location information. This data is only used for display purposes and is not collected or stored in any form. The only exception is the odometer value, which is saved in the device's internal storage. The odometer can be reset in the app's settings.
