Location Permission

General

Location permission is used to access the device's GPS sensor to calculate and display your real-time speed. Unless Premium+ Navigator is active, this data is processed locally on your device and is not collected or stored in any form. The only exception is the odometer value, which is saved locally on the device and can be reset at any time in the app's settings.

Premium+ Navigator only

By enabling background trip recording feature a notification will be displayed and the location will be accessed even when you enter another application or turn off your screen. By clicking the "Stop" button in the notification or by closing the app from recents the current location will no longer be used by the app.
You may disable background trip recording from the Quick Menu, in which case location access occurs only when the app is visible.

Every app session records a trip containing all the location data, which is saved locally in the internal storage.
You may view or delete saved trips in the Trips Panel. Deleting the app also deletes all locally stored trips.

When the map is displayed, location data is encrypted and transmitted via Google Play Services to determine which area of the map to show. This behavior is handled according to https://policies.google.com/privacy.
