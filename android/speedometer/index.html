GPS Speedometer Premium_

Accurate, ad-free GPS speedometer — simple, straightforward, and feature-packed.

GPS Speedometer Premium is a mobile application designed to provide accurate and reliable speed measurements using the Global Positioning System (GPS) technology. Whether you're commuting to work, embarking on a road trip, or simply curious about your current speed, this app provides a reliable and comprehensive solution for all your speed measurement needs.

Highlighted features:

  • HUD mode
  • Heading direction compass
  • Different tacho scales
  • Coordinates and altitude display
  • G-Force meter
  • Roll & pitch widget
  • Audible / visual speed alert
  • Color palette
  • And many more
